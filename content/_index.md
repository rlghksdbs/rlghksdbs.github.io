# Kihwan Yoon

AI Researcher at **BLUEDOT**, working on computer vision and on-device deep learning. I received my Ph.D. from the **University of Seoul** in 2024, advised by Prof. Yong Han Kim.

{{< social >}}

**Research Interests**
- Real-Time Super-Resolution
- Video Frame Interpolation
- Stereoscopic Imaging & HDR
- On-device LLM & Image/Video Restoration
